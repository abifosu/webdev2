When you apply display: flex or display: grid to a, it continues to behave like a block element, using display: block. However, it changes the way its child elements behave. For example, with just display: flex (and no other Flexbox-related properties) applied to the parent, its children will distribute themselves horizontally. Or, to put it another way, the flow direction is switched from vertical to horizontal.
